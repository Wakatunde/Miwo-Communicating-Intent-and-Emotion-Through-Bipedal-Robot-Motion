
\chapter{Discussion}
This chapter aims to discuss and interpret the findings presented in the results chapter while also examining them in the context of the research objectives defined in Chapter 1 of the thesis. 
This section will initially examine the emotion recognition results, looking further into which animations succeeded, which fell short, and what this means for the goal of communicating emotion through biped motion. Furthermore, the Likert and Godspeed results are discussed, with an emphasis on how the overall perception of the robot informs its suitability for human-robot interaction. Where relevant, differences between video-based and live results are examined, and possible explanations are considered.
